About The Irie Room

Founded on the principles of community, quality, and the Rastafarian way of life, The Irie Room is more than just a smoke shop. We're a gathering place for like-minded individuals who appreciate the finer things in life.

Our name "Irie" comes from the Jamaican Patois word meaning "good" or "pleasant" - and that's exactly what we strive to provide to every customer who walks through our doors.

Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or new to the culture, our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you and help you find exactly what you're looking for.
